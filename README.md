NovaPay Risk Dashboard

A professional dashboard for monitoring compliance and risk metrics.  
Designed to help organizations visualize key risk indicators, streamline reporting, and support governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) decision-making.

2. Explore interactive charts and risk metrics.  
3. Review the risk register for detailed entries.
